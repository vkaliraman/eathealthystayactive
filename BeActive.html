Ways to increase physical activity

Walk up and down the soccer or softball field sidelines while watching the kids play. Here is a list of other activities you can do to increase your physical activity.

Vigorous Physical Activities

Swimming, bicycling, aerobics, basketball, weighlifting, heavy yard work are all some of the many vigorous physical activites you can do to stay healthy.

Bicycling

Bicyling at more than 10 miles/hour is a vigorous physical activity. A 5' 10" man weighing 154-pound will use up 590 calories bicycling for an hour at that speed.
